About Me

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science at Center for Research and Advanced Studies of the National Polytechnic Institute (Guadalajara). My research interests include Deep Learning, High Performance Computing, Complex Networks, Access Control, Graph Mining, and Machine Learning. I enjoy developing scalable software for data-intensive applications and GPU-based computing using CUDA.
